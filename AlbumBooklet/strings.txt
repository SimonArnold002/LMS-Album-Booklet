PLUGIN_ALBUMBOOKLET
	EN	Album Booklet

PLUGIN_ALBUMBOOKLET_DESC
	EN	Adds a "View Booklet" entry to Material Skin's Now Playing menu that opens a PDF booklet found in the currently-playing album's folder.

PLUGIN_ALBUMBOOKLET_VIEW
	EN	View Booklet

PLUGIN_ALBUMBOOKLET_CHOOSE
	EN	Choose a booklet

PLUGIN_ALBUMBOOKLET_NONE
	EN	No booklet (PDF) found in this album's folder.

PLUGIN_ALBUMBOOKLET_NO_TRACK
	EN	No local album is playing. A booklet can only be shown for a track from your library.

PLUGIN_ALBUMBOOKLET_MATERIAL
	EN	Show "View Booklet" on Now Playing

PLUGIN_ALBUMBOOKLET_MATERIAL_DESC
	EN	Add the "View Booklet" entry to Material Skin's Now Playing "…" menu. After changing this, hard-refresh Material once (it caches custom actions when the app loads).

PLUGIN_ALBUMBOOKLET_OPENMODE
	EN	Open booklet in

PLUGIN_ALBUMBOOKLET_OPENMODE_DESC
	EN	Whether the PDF opens inside a Material dialog (in-app) or in a new browser tab.

PLUGIN_ALBUMBOOKLET_MODE_IFRAME
	EN	In-app dialog (iframe)

PLUGIN_ALBUMBOOKLET_MODE_WEBLINK
	EN	New browser tab

PLUGIN_ALBUMBOOKLET_HINT
	EN	The entry always appears while a track is playing; if the album folder has no PDF it shows a short "no booklet" message. Booklets are read from local library folders only.
